Factiva Newspages

Module Directory

  • 1.0 RSS Displays lists of articles generated by RSS.
  • 2.0 Radar Displays indication bars that represent number of articles for that company/industry/subject. Shows total as well as percent of change in news volume.
  • 3.0 News Stand Displays sections of sources with an article list for each source. Also shows topics that surface in articles as well as an article source carousel.
  • 4.0 Regional Map Displays volume of articles represented by size of bubble. User can filter volume by selecting subjects. These are the top 10-20 Subjects determined by news volume.
  • 5.0 Custom Topic Displays sections of article lists.
  • 6.0 Top News Displays recent headlines, latest audio & video, and an opinion & analysis column by author.
  • 7.0 Alerts Displays personal and shared article alerts.
  • 8.0 Industry Displays top 5 mentioned companies/people/subjects based on news volume. Also shows the largest increase and decrease in news volume as a percentage.
  • 9.0 Company Profile Displays company information such as news volume and stock price graph, current price and analysis reports, trend bubbles and recent articles.
  • 10.0 Summary Displays a summary of information for that topic. Article headlines by time, video content, a list of trending people and companies, a news volume & market index graph, and a map of news by region.
  • 11.0 Sources Displays sections of sources with an article list for each source.
  • Popup Interactions Displays popup content types: Trend, Company, Source, Person, Subject, Keyword, and Author.
